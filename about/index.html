Akira Moroo

I am a PhD student at the University of Tokyo. My current research field is firmware security, especially x86 and RISC-V. I am also interested in operating systems, and compilers. Previously I have researched in Natural Language Processing and Software Engineering. You can find my e-mail address on the bottom of this page. Please do not hesitate to contact me.
